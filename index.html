About the Project

We are partnering with the Vision Aid Digital Accessibility Testing Team to build LLM-powered tools that automate digital accessibility analysis and remediation. Our system analyzes webpage source code, identifies WCAG compliance issues, and generates structured reports with proposed fixes.
